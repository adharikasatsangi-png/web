So, I love eating Jalebi and Gulab Jamun

Jalebi
A sweet made by deep-frying maida flour batter in circular shapes and then soaked in sugar syrup.
Gulab Jamun
A sweet made from milk solids, traditionally from khoya, which is kneaded into a dough, shaped into small balls, deep-fried, and soaked in sugar syrup flavored with cardamom, rose water, saffron, or kewra.
Rasgulla
A syrupy dessert popular in the Indian subcontinent and regions with South Asian diasporas. It is made from ball-shaped dumplings of chhena (an Indian cottage cheese) and semolina dough, cooked in light syrup made of sugar.
